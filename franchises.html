7-Eleven

Known for its round-the-clock convenience, 7-Eleven is a global chain that offers a variety of snacks, beverages, and essential household items. It is particularly famous for its Slurpee frozen drinks and Big Gulp beverages. The franchise operates in numerous countries, providing a quick stop for customers needing last-minute items or a quick snack.

Visit 7-Eleven for more information.

Gino's

Gino's Burgers & Chicken, originally known as Gino's Hamburgers, was founded in 1957 by Baltimore Colts football players Gino Marchetti and Alan Ameche, along with their friend Louis C. Fischer. The chain, popular in the Mid-Atlantic U.S., was known for its burgers and as the original franchisee of Kentucky Fried Chicken in the region. Famous for the "Gino Giant" burger, Gino's competed with major fast food chains until it was absorbed by Marriott Corporation in 1982, though it has since made a comeback with a nostalgic appeal.

Visit Gino's for more information.
